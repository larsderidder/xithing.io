What we do

We build a better Internet of Things. Safe, simple and self-reliant. Improving both your business and our lives. This is how we contribute to Web11, the better web. The Kunnis Manifesto. serves as our compass. XIThing is a member of the Media2B group.

Make things smart & connected

We are good at making anything smart and connect them to a larger network. Anything from lamp posts to bicycles, and from shopping carts to airplanes. You're a producer of design bikes and want to turn them into smart, connected digital citizens? XIThing is that rare company that can help you with every aspect of the project. From thinking up useful use cases to implementing the software stack, and from running the project as an efficient agile project to designing API's to expose the functionality of a device. XIThing's team of experienced engineers know how to deliver value on time to both you and the rest of the world.

Improving business processes with smart devices

Many business processes can be improved, and many new ones created, by the smart application of IoT technologies. Used correctly they can make processes faster, safer, cheaper and more predictable. XIThing's unique process-driven modelling approach ensures that IoT technologies are only applied to those steps in a process where they make sense and add value.

Device Security

When making connecting smart things with each other, security is your number one responsibility. With HaXIT XIThing will offer a platform where the world's best hardware hackers help you with making sure your IoT enabled device is as secure as possible. It's the easiest, fastest and best way to make your devices IoT proof!

About us

  • 2015

    Our Humble Beginnings

    Inspired by the ideal of Web11 a couple of hard- and software enthusiasts decided that is was time to work on the physical part of the better web Web11 strives for. With a combined total of a couple of decades of experience in both hard- and software engineering they had been waiting for that day to come since the nineties. It wasn't until 2015 that the needed technologies for building that better physical web emerged. The founders decided it was time to build cool stuff that mattered, with the immensely valuable expertise within the Media2B group. The shared Web11 vision made the match complete and in 2015 they kicked off XIThing.

  • November 2015

    Green houses

    The first XIThing project immediately showed that XIThing was serious about building a better world by working on Web11. This initial project was about making houses smarter with regards to energy consumption by the clever use of IoT technologies. Starting with creating insight into past usage, XIThing continued with modelling the usage profile of the house and analysing where which IoT technologies could improve the energy consumption.
